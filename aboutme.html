Meet Marlena: teacher of accessible queer dance

About me

Marlena Fraune (they/she) has been dancing since 2009 in a variety of styles, as a leader, follower, and switch dancer. They have won ballroom dance competitions in Colorado, New Mexico, and St Louis. They have completed teacher training in 9 dances, taught in more, and learned and danced around the world.

They modify their teaching to suit each individual student's wants and needs. This includes making accommodations like dancing some or all of the dance while seated, or supplementing instruction with ASL. Their teaching is informed by somatic techniques such as Alexander Technique, stretches, and strengthening exercises.
